Article accepted by *INFORMS Journal on Computing*.

- Coauthor: Yu Yang
- DOI: [10.1287/ijoc.2025.1415](https://pubsonline.informs.org/doi/10.1287/ijoc.2025.1415)
- Repository: [GitHub](https://github.com/Zhengzhong-You/RouteOpt)
- Documentation: [RouteOpt Docs](https://zhengzhong-you.github.io/RouteOpt-Docs/)

RouteOpt is an open-source modular exact solver for vehicle routing problems. The project emphasizes branch-price-and-cut, parallel pricing, learning-augmented branching, reusable solver components, and reproducible computational workflows for exact optimization research.
