Published in *Operations Research*.

- Coauthors: Yu Yang, Xinshang Wang, and Wotao Yin
- DOI: [10.1287/opre.2023.0615](https://pubsonline.informs.org/doi/10.1287/opre.2023.0615)

This article studies how learning can be incorporated into branch-price-and-cut algorithms for solving vehicle routing problems exactly. It develops a two-stage learning-to-branch framework and reports substantial runtime reductions on standard benchmark classes, including strong gains relative to VRPSolver.
