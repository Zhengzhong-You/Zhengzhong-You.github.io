I am interested in collaborating on problems at the intersection of exact optimization, learning-augmented algorithms, and deployable decision-support tools.

Current collaboration interests include:

- branch-price-and-cut and column-generation methods
- learning to accelerate exact optimization
- routing, scheduling, and inventory systems
- open-source solver engineering and reproducible computation

If your work overlaps with these directions, feel free to reach out by email.
