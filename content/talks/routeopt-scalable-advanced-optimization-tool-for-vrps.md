This presentation introduced RouteOpt as an open-source exact solver for vehicle routing problems with a strong emphasis on modularity, solver extensibility, and reproducible computational research. The talk focused on why open and high-performing solver infrastructure matters for the field, especially when researchers need a platform that can support customized branching, cutting, and algorithmic experimentation.

The presentation also highlighted RouteOpt's exact-solution performance, its reusable architecture, and its role as a public research tool rather than a one-off codebase attached to a single paper.
