The paper *Two-Stage Learning to Branch in Branch-Price-and-Cut Algorithms for Solving Vehicle Routing Problems Exactly* was accepted by *Operations Research* on January 14, 2026.

Paper focus: learning-augmented branching for exact vehicle-routing algorithms in the branch-price-and-cut setting.

Published online in Articles in Advance on February 24, 2026.

- DOI: [10.1287/opre.2023.0615](https://doi.org/10.1287/opre.2023.0615)
